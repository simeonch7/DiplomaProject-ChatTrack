Welcome to my diploma project!

This project is centered around the important task of monitoring communication channels.

In today's world, communication is crucial in virtually every aspect of life - from personal relationships to business operations. With so many different channels available, it can be difficult to keep track of all the information that is being shared. That's where my project comes in.

By monitoring communication channels, the system will be able to identify potential issues or problems that may arise.

Overall, this project has the potential to be a valuable tool for anyone who wants to better understand the communication landscape. Whether you are an individual looking to improve your personal relationships, or a business looking to stay ahead of the competition, my project can help you achieve your goals. Thank you for taking the time to learn about my project, and I hope you find it as useful and informative as I do!

- Simeon Chakarov, student at TU-Sofia
